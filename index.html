Hi, I'm Zack

I’m the creator of Z Best Goodies, a bakery focused on making delicious, allergen-friendly treats that everyone can enjoy safely.

My goal is to provide baked goods that are free from the top common allergens while keeping taste and quality a top priority. Everything is made in carefully separated kitchens to prevent cross-contamination, so customers can feel confident every time they order.

I also want to make the experience easy and convenient through simple ordering and allergy tracking, so getting your favorite treats is stress-free.

At its core, Z Best Goodies is about safe, high-quality desserts and bringing people together over something sweet.
